Darts Log for Russ Bray Darts Scorer

 

This application can handle mails sent by the iPhone/iPod-Application "Russ Bray Darts Scorer"
  1. Send the email from iPad/iPhone-App to your mail-account
  2. Mark (select this and all other mails containing darts-scores)
  3. Choose Export from Mail-Programm and export all Mails to a single text-file
  4. Upload this file to this application
  5. See the results formated as a table
  6. Download your results as CSV-file
